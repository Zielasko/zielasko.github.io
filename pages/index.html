About

I am a PhD student at the University of Bremen and a researcher at the Cyber-Physical Systems department of the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence (DFKI). My research focuses on Virtual Prototype-driven tracing, analysis, verification and hardware optimization.

Topics of Interest RISC-V, Virtual Prototyping, System-level Analysis, Visualization, Formal Verification, Symbolic Execution, Hardware Optimization, AI-Accelerator Design, Reverse Engineering
